About Learn Academy
A coaching centre built on the belief that every student deserves quality education, personalised attention, and a path forward.

Education That Shapes Futures

Learn Academy Dalkhola was founded with a clear mission: to bring high-quality academic coaching to the students of North Dinajpur and surrounding regions. We bridge the gap between classroom learning and exam success, offering structured programmes designed for real outcomes.

From foundational schooling support for Classes 1–12 to professional programmes like IELTS, CTET, and D.El.Ed, our faculty brings expertise, patience, and a genuine commitment to student growth.

Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before joining Learn Academy Dalkhola.
What are the batch timings at Learn Academy?
+
We run morning, afternoon, and evening batches to suit school-going students, working professionals, and college students. Typical slots are 7–9 AM, 12–2 PM, and 5–7 PM. Exact timings are confirmed at enrollment based on your availability.
What are the course fees? Are there any concessions?
+
Fees vary by programme and batch size. We offer merit-based concessions and sibling discounts. Please contact us directly or submit an enquiry to receive a personalised fee structure. We believe no deserving student should be turned away due to financial constraints.
Do you provide study materials?
+
Yes. All students receive curated notes, worksheets, and previous year question papers relevant to their course. For UGC NET and CTET, we provide PYQ-based practice sets (2004–2024). IELTS students get full-length mock tests modelled on real exam formats.
Can I enroll in more than one course simultaneously?
+
Absolutely. Many students combine School Coaching with Spoken English, or D.El.Ed support with CTET preparation. We help you build a non-conflicting timetable. A small combo discount may also apply — ask us during counselling.
Is there an online learning option available?
+
We currently operate primarily as an in-person centre at Dalkhola. However, hybrid options (recorded sessions + WhatsApp support) are available for students who cannot attend in person on certain days. Enquire with us for details.
How do I enroll? What documents are needed?
+
Fill out our online enquiry form or visit us directly at our Dalkhola centre. Documents typically required: a passport-sized photo, a copy of your last marksheet or admit card, and an Aadhaar card for identity. Enrollment is confirmed on payment of the first installment.
