Our Story

About Learn Academy

A coaching centre built on the belief that every student deserves quality education, personalised attention, and a path forward.

Who We Are

Education That Shapes Futures

Learn Academy Dalkhola was founded with a clear mission: to bring high-quality academic coaching to the students of North Dinajpur and surrounding regions. We bridge the gap between classroom learning and exam success, offering structured programmes designed for real outcomes.

From foundational schooling support for Classes 1–12 to professional programmes like IELTS, UGC NET, and D.El.Ed, our faculty brings expertise, patience, and a genuine commitment to student growth.

All Programs

What We Offer

📚
School Coaching (1–12)
All Boards

Subject-wise expert coaching for students from Class 1 through Class 12, covering all major boards with regular assessments and report cards.

  • English, Mathematics, Science
  • Social Studies & Languages
  • Periodic tests & worksheets
  • AI-generated report cards
🗣️
Spoken English
All Levels

Build fluency, confidence, and real-world communication skills through structured conversation practice, grammar, and pronunciation training.

  • Beginner to Advanced levels
  • Grammar & vocabulary building
  • Conversation & debate practice
  • Pronunciation & accent training
✈️
IELTS Coaching
General & Academic

Targeted IELTS preparation covering all four modules — Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking — with full-length mock tests and feedback.

  • All 4 modules covered
  • Full mock test practice
  • Band-score strategy sessions
  • Speaking interview prep
🎓
UGC NET English
Competitive Exam

Comprehensive preparation for UGC NET English Literature using previous year questions (2004–2024), concept clarity, and exam strategy.

  • PYQ-based practice (2004–2024)
  • Shakespeare, Chaucer & more
  • Literary theory & criticism
  • Paper I (General) preparation
📝
D.El.Ed Support
WB D.El.Ed

Guidance and academic support for WB D.El.Ed students including lesson plan preparation, practicum support, and assignment help.

  • Lesson plan templates (NCFTE-2009)
  • Practicum & internship guidance
  • Assignment & project support
  • Exam preparation
⭐
Personalised Tutoring
One-on-One

Custom one-on-one sessions for students who need focused attention on specific subjects or exam preparation at their own pace.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Customised study plan
  • Any subject / any grade
  • Progress tracked weekly
